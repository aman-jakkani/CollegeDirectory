Instructions

Copy paste the URL given for each functionality, including your input as a parameter wherever you see {}.


Is my professor tough or not?

Search a professor's average grade! - http://localhost:8080/toughOrNot/{professor_id}


Which sponsors do I as a student have available?

Get a table with your sponsors here! - http://localhost:8080/sponsors/{student_id}


I am a mentor! Who are my mentees?

As a professor, you can get a table with your student mentees here! - http://localhost:8080/mentees/{professor_id}
